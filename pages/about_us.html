Our Heritage

From Naples to the Heart of Dhaka

Ciao Dhaka was born from a singular passion: to redefine the pizza experience in our city. We didn't just want to serve food; we wanted to transport our guests to a rustic corner of Tuscany, where time slows down and flavor takes center stage.

The Alchemy of Ingredients

Golden Truffle Essence

We source our truffle oil directly from the Umbria region. This liquid gold adds a complex, earthy dimension to our signature pies, creating a sensory bridge between Italian soil and Dhaka's vibrant palate.

Living Dough

Our dough undergoes a 48-hour slow fermentation process, using only '00' flour and natural yeast. The result? A crust that is light, airy, and remarkably digestible.
